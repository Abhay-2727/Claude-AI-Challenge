💡 One thing I learned while building this simulator:
Prior Authorization isn't simply about getting permission—it's about proving medical necessity with complete and accurate documentation.
Even a clinically appropriate treatment can be delayed if supporting evidence is incomplete. Good workflow design isn't just about moving tasks forward; 
it's about reducing friction while ensuring every stakeholder has the information they need to make the right decision.
That insight completely changed how I think about workflow automation. Sometimes the biggest challenge isn't writing code—it's understanding the 
real-world process you're trying to improve
