💡 What I Learned About AI Skills

The biggest value wasn't the report itself.

It was creating a repeatable framework that forces AI to:

• Verify data sources
• Follow a consistent checklist
• Highlight both strengths and risks
• Avoid speculation
• Present balanced conclusions

A well-designed skill transforms AI from a chatbot into a specialized research assistant.
