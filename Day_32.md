💡 Biggest Lesson About Audience-First Marketing

The most valuable insight I gained was:
Marketing isn't about creating more content—it's about creating the right content for the right audience on the right platform.
When you truly understand your audience, choosing platforms, defining content pillars, and building a growth roadmap become strategic decisions rather than guesswork.
