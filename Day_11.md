💡 What I Learned
A strong resume is not about adding fancy designs or stuffing keywords everywhere.
It's about presenting your existing experience in a way that is:
✅ ATS-friendly
✅ Recruiter-friendly
✅ Keyword-aligned
✅ Easy to scan
✅ Relevant to the target role

The goal isn't to change your story.

The goal is to communicate it better.
