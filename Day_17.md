 🎯 Analysis Findings

One of the most surprising insights from the vehicle analysis was the E85 Paradox. Although E85 fuel was around 18% cheaper than petrol at the pump, 
its lower mileage resulted in a 3.6% higher running cost per kilometer. The dashboard visualizations made this trade-off immediately visible, showing 
that fuel price alone can be misleading. This project reinforced the importance of analyzing multiple factors—cost, efficiency, maintenance, 
and emissions—before making data-driven decisions.

🎯 Learning: 
The biggest lesson from today's project wasn't about fuel.
It was about analytics.
If you only track one metric, you can make bad decisions.
When you combine:
✔ Cost
 ✔ Efficiency
 ✔ Maintenance
 ✔ Environmental impact
 ✔ Vehicle age
You get a much more complete picture.
And that's exactly what data analysts are supposed to do.
Transform raw data into decisions.
