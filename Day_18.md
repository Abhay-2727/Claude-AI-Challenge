💡 lesson:
The quality of an AI-generated report depends heavily on how well the prompt defines structure and constraints. 
By explicitly telling Claude not to assume missing information and to preserve ownership, dates, and terminology exactly as provided, 
the output became far more reliable and professional.

What stood out most was seeing an unstructured meeting transcript transformed into something that looked and 
felt like a modern project management dashboard—similar to Notion, ClickUp, or Asana.

AI isn't just helping us generate content anymore—it's helping us organize thinking, improve accountability,
and reduce information overload.
