🔥 What I Learned

Most applicants submit the same resume everywhere and hope for the best. 
Top candidates create a strategy.
A recruiter might first see: 
➡️ Your LinkedIn Profile 
➡️ Your Connection Request 
➡️ Your Resume 
➡️ Your Cover Letter 
➡️ Your Follow-Up Message 
➡️ Your Interview Introduction 
If each of those communicates a different message, your personal brand becomes weak. 
Today's exercise showed me how important it is to maintain consistency across every touchpoint.
