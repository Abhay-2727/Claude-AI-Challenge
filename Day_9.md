✅ comparison notes
The MVP provides a functional nutrition tracking dashboard focused on daily nutrient monitoring.
The Enhanced Version transforms NutriScope into a more complete nutrition intelligence platform by introducing 
meal planning, risk assessment, broader nutrient coverage, data import capabilities, and advanced recommendation systems,
resulting in significantly greater analytical depth and user value.
